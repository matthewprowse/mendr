## Lint baseline (pre-audit)

The codebase enters the audit with a red lint state. Many of these problems will likely surface as audit findings (forbidden patterns, file-too-large complaints triggering rules, etc.). Rather than pause the audit to fix lint as a separate project, we treat the current counts as a **regression budget**:

- **Pre-audit:** 49 errors, 55 warnings.
- **Rule for every Wave B phase:** the post-phase counts must be **≤ baseline**. Any phase that introduces a new error or warning halts the wave.

This is a strictly conservative budget — phases are encouraged to drive the counts down but never up.
